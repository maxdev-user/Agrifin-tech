Our Partnership Ecosystem

We mobilize capital and market access through strategic alliances

Commercial Banks

Co-lend to farmers and SMEs through our managed services model, reducing risk via Turmi’s oversight of production and offtake.

Impact Investors

Provide catalytic capital (debt/equity/PRI) that unlocks matching funds from local institutions and de-risks livestock value chains.

Buyer Financing

Processors and exporters pre-finance production in exchange for exclusive offtake of live animals or meat, ensuring quality and traceability.

Technology and cold-chain partners enable traceability, certification, and efficient delivery to domestic and international markets.
